# PGConnect-A-Location-Based-PG-Roommate-Discovery-and-Review-Platform
PGConnect is a location-based platform to find PG accommodations and compatible roommates. Users can search PGs, post roommate requirements, switch roommates, contact owners, and view verified reviews—making PG living transparent, safe, and hassle-free.


# PGConnect – PG & Roommate Discovery Platform

PGConnect is a location-based web application that helps students and working professionals find Paying Guest (PG) accommodations and compatible roommates. The platform also allows PG owners to list properties and enables users to share verified, honest reviews.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Finding a suitable PG or a compatible roommate is often difficult due to:
- Lack of trusted platforms
- Fake listings and biased reviews
- Difficulty in replacing or switching roommates
- No direct communication channel with PG owners

PGConnect solves these problems by providing a transparent, centralized, and user-friendly platform.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Help users find PGs near their city or college
- Enable users to search and match with compatible roommates
- Allow tenants to replace or switch roommates easily
- Provide verified and honest PG reviews
- Connect PG owners directly with potential tenants

---

## 🧩 System Users

1. **Students / Tenants**
2. **Room Owners / Existing Tenants**
3. **PG Owners / Hostel Managers**

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 PG Search & Discovery
- Search PGs by city or location
- Filter by price, amenities, gender, and sharing type
- View PG photos and details
- Contact PG owners directly

### 👥 Roommate Finder
- Post room availability or roommate requirements
- Match roommates based on budget, lifestyle, and location
- Private user-to-user communication

### 🔁 Roommate / Room Change
- Request to replace or switch roommates
- Find nearby compatible roommates

### ⭐ Reviews & Ratings
- Verified tenant reviews
- Ratings for cleanliness, food, safety, owner behavior
- Fake review reporting system

### 🏢 PG Owner Panel
- Add and manage PG listings
- Update room availability
- Respond to reviews
- Receive tenant inquiries
